What is HarvardKey?

HarvardKey is the University's new unified credential for access to a wealth of online resources spanning the Harvard Community. Users across Harvard will be invited to claim a HarvardKey over the fall and winter of 2015 for simpler, more secure access to the applications and services you use every day. If you haven't yet received your invitation, simply choose the appropriate tab at the top of this login screen for your usual login type.
